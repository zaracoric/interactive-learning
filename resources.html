Educational Resources

These tools, websites, and platforms will help you continue learning, practicing, and exploring math and technology concepts.

    Video tutorials and exercises for math, computing, science, and more.
  • Codecademy
    Learn programming languages like Python, JavaScript, and HTML/CSS interactively.
  • Photomath
    Scan math problems to see instant step-by-step solutions.
  • GeoGebra
    Visual tools for algebra, geometry, and graphing.
  • PhET Interactive Simulations
    Free math and science simulations created by the University of Colorado Boulder.
